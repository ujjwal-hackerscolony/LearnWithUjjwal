Biology Chapter 1: The Living World

Introduction

Biology science ki wo branch hai jo living organisms ka study karti hai. Earth par bahut types ke living organisms paye jate hain jaise plants, animals aur microorganisms.

Characteristics of Living Organisms

Living organisms me kuch special characteristics hoti hain:

  • Growth – Living organisms grow karte hain.
  • Reproduction – Living organisms apni species ko continue karne ke liye reproduce karte hain.
  • Metabolism – Body ke andar chemical reactions hote hain.
  • Response to Stimuli – Living organisms environment ke changes par react karte hain.

Diversity in Living World

Earth par millions of species exist karti hain. Inhe study aur samajhne ke liye scientists classification system ka use karte hain.

Importance of Biology

Biology ka knowledge agriculture, medicine aur environment protection me bahut important hai.
