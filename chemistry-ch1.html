Chemistry Chapter 1: Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry

Introduction

Chemistry wo science hai jo matter ki properties, composition aur changes ko study karti hai. Har wo cheez jisme mass hota hai aur jo space occupy karti hai use matter kehte hain.

Types of Matter

Matter ko mainly teen states me divide kiya jata hai:

  • Solid – jiska shape aur volume fixed hota hai.
  • Liquid – jiska volume fixed hota hai par shape container par depend karta hai.
  • Gas – jiska na shape fixed hota hai na volume.

Law of Conservation of Mass

Is law ke according chemical reaction me mass na create hota hai aur na destroy hota hai. Total mass reaction se pehle aur baad me same rehta hai.

Importance of Chemistry

Chemistry ka use medicines, fertilizers, fuels aur daily life ke bahut saare products banane me hota hai.
