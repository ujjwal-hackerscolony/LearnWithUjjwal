Class 11 Biology Notes

1. The Living World

Biology science ki wo branch hai jo living organisms ka study karti hai. Living organisms grow karte hain, reproduce karte hain aur environment ke changes par response dete hain.

2. Biological Classification

Biological classification ka matlab hai living organisms ko different groups me divide karna. Isse organisms ko samajhna aur study karna easy ho jata hai.

3. Plant Kingdom

Plant kingdom me sabhi plants aate hain. Plants photosynthesis karte hain aur oxygen produce karte hain. Plants environment ke liye bahut important hote hain.

4. Animal Kingdom

Animal kingdom me sabhi animals aate hain. Animals apna food khud nahi bana sakte, isliye wo plants ya dusre animals par depend karte hain.
