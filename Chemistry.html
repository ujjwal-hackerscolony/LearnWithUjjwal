Class 11 Chemistry Notes

1. Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry

Chemistry science ki ek branch hai jo matter ki composition, structure aur properties ko study karti hai. Matter wo sab kuch hota hai jisme mass hota hai aur jo space occupy karta hai.

2. Laws of Chemical Combination

Law of Conservation of Mass ke according chemical reaction me mass na create hota hai aur na destroy hota hai. Mass hamesha constant rehta hai.

3. Structure of Atom

Atom matter ka sabse chhota particle hota hai. Atom ke andar proton, neutron aur electron hote hain. Proton positive charge rakhta hai, electron negative charge aur neutron neutral hota hai.

4. Importance of Chemistry

Chemistry daily life me bahut important hai. Medicines, food preservation, fertilizers aur fuels sab chemistry ki help se bante hain.
