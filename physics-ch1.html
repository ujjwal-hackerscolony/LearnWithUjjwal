Physics Chapter 1: Units and Measurements

Introduction

Physics me measurement bahut important hota hai. Kisi bhi physical quantity ko measure karne ke liye hume ek unit ki zarurat hoti hai. Measurement ka matlab hota hai kisi quantity ko ek standard unit se compare karna.

Physical Quantities

Physical quantities wo quantities hoti hain jise measure kiya ja sakta hai. Jaise length, mass, time, temperature etc.

SI Units

SI system duniya ka standard system hai. Isme kuch important units hain:

  • Length – meter (m)
  • Mass – kilogram (kg)
  • Time – second (s)
  • Temperature – kelvin (K)
  • Electric current – ampere (A)

Importance of Measurement

Accurate measurement science aur technology me bahut important hota hai. Isse experiments sahi tarike se perform kiye ja sakte hain.
